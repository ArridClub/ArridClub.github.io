About the Arrid Club

A Home for Recovery Since 1973

Our Mission

The Arrid Club is a non-profit corporation whose primary purpose is to provide and maintain a facility for its members and other attendees of 12-step programs so they may hold meetings and host social and recreational events. We are committed to supporting the recovery community of the San Jacinto Valley with a safe, welcoming, and accessible space every day of the week.

🏛️ Our History

The Arrid Club has been serving the San Jacinto Valley since June 20, 1973. For over five decades, we have provided a home for the recovery community — a place where those seeking sobriety can find fellowship, support, and hope.

🤝 Who We Serve

Our doors are open to members of Alcoholics Anonymous, Narcotics Anonymous, and all 12-step programs. We welcome anyone who is committed to recovery, regardless of where they are on their journey.

📅 Meetings & Events

We host AA and NA meetings daily starting at 6:00 AM, as well as monthly social and recreational events including breakfasts, dances, golf outings, BBQs, and workshops for our members and the broader community.
