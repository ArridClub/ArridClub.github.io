A Home for Recovery Since 1973

The Arrid Club has been a cornerstone of the San Jacinto Valley recovery community since June 20, 1973. We provide a safe, welcoming facility where members of Alcoholics Anonymous, Narcotics Anonymous, and other 12-step programs can gather, heal, and support one another.

Our doors are open every day of the week, with meetings starting as early as 6:00 AM. Whether you're new to recovery or have years of sobriety, you'll find a community here.
